KNOWABILITY

Knowability is a platform committed to empowering differently-abled individuals through education, community support, interactive games, and quizzes designed for self-assessment.

Our Features

Educational Guidance

Knowability's educational guidance is a multifaceted approach to learning, encompassing engaging videos, interactive games, and challenging quizzes. These resources provide a comprehensive and enjoyable learning experience, catering to diverse learning styles and preferences. Whether you prefer visual content, hands-on activities, or self-assessment, Knowability's platform has you covered, ensuring an enriching educational journey for all.

Community

Knowability fosters a sense of belonging and support through various dedicated communities where individuals can openly communicate, share their challenges, and seek advice from their peers. These communities serve as safe spaces for meaningful interactions, allowing members to connect, empathize, and collaborate with one another. It's a platform that values the power of community and encourages individuals to come together, making learning and personal growth a collective experience.

NGO's

At Knowability, we have partnered with reputable NGOs to initiate impactful campaigns aimed at raising awareness about the challenges faced by the disabled community and advocating for solutions to mitigate these issues. Through these collaborations, we strive to shed light on the importance of inclusivity and accessibility in society. By joining hands with NGOs, we aim to inspire positive change and contribute to a more inclusive world where every individual, regardless of their abilities, can thrive and lead fulfilling lives.

Games and Quizes

At Knowability, we offer a range of engaging games and quizzes designed to empower individuals. These interactive activities not only provide enjoyment but also serve as valuable tools for self-assessment and skill enhancement. With our leaderboard feature, users can track their progress and compare their performance with others, fostering a sense of healthy competition and motivation for personal growth. Through games, quizzes, and leaderboards, we create an environment where learning and self-improvement go hand in hand, making education an enjoyable and rewarding experience.
