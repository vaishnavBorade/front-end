Sarthak Educational Trust

Sarthak envisions an India where PwDs live with dignity and work towards this through various interventions. The last 15 years have been dedicated to the same aim and now all set to dedicate the next 25 years to the empowerment of people with disabilities. In this series, Sarthak already started innovating new solutions such as- CapSarathi - Mobile Application, RozgarSarathi- Exclusive job portal for PwDs and upcoming projects namely- Sarthak Global Resource Centre- First-of its-kind building for Pwds and GyanSarathi- E-LMS.

Sounds of Silence is an initiative, straight from the heart of a chosen few who wish to fill the lives of million hearing-impaired kids across cities, borders, nations with the beauty of expression and art of communication.

Sense India's key objective is to provide information, guidance, support and training needed along with being committed to supporting persons with deafblindness and multiple disabilities and their families by raising awareness and campaigning for their rights, opportunities and services throughout the nation.
